> Starting Over ... again

Posted by Christopher Swayne on Thursday, March 12, 2020

So I’ve been absent from updating my website as of late. The biggest issue concerning this was that I had upgraded from a 2011 MacBook Pro, to a 2015 MacBook Pro. Sidenote: I’d be willing to debate this, but the 2015 MacBook Pro was the last “great” MacBook Whilst doing this, I ended up committing the grave sin that all people do at some point or another- I never backed up the GoHugo site-generator source code. This essentially limited my ability to rebulid the site from scratch at an identical level. The many attempts I had, all failed, so instead of trying to rebuild the site from the same and (unfortunately) broken theme, I switched to a new theme and configured the site many times easier, with the config.toml and README.md being quite legible and simplistic. I hope that the new site is pleasing to you all and your eyes.

Now, on to what I’ve been doing:

  • Installed FreeBSD on my secondary laptop
  • Developed a few C# applications for some practice
  • Installed a DashCam on my Isuzu Rodeo, and preparing for modifications
  • Began Development of an Android App for a course at UMKC

Future Plans:

  • Finish updating the site to match the aesthetic that I prefer
  • Finish Android App
